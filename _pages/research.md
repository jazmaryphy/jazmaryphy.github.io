- Density Functional Theory (DFT)
- Computational Materials Science

# Academic Experience & Projects

### ❖ 2022–2024: Postdoctoral Researcher
**Quantum Materials Group (Prof. Samuelle Sanna)** | *Università di Bologna, Italy*
<br>Project focus: *"Using $\mu$SR and/or NMR to probe novel quantum phenomena"*

* <span style="color: #c00000;">✔</span> **Spin dynamics in Lanthanide nanomagnets** — In collaboration with Prof. Alessandro Lascialfari (Pavia) & Prof. Lorenzo Sorace (Firenze).
* <span style="color: #c00000;">✔</span> **Role of spin-orbit coupling in Re-based double perovskites** — In collaboration with Prof. Cesare Franchini (Bologna).
* <span style="color: #c00000;">✔</span> **Gap magnets** — In collaboration with Prof. Giuseppe Allodi (Parma).

### ❖ 2019–2022: Ph.D. in Physics
*Università di Parma, Italy*
<br>Thesis: *"Muon sites and couplings in magnetic and superconducting materials: towards high-throughput modelling"* under the supervision of Prof. Roberto De Renzi.

* <span style="color: #c00000;">✔</span> Developed a high-throughput DFT approach to automate muon stopping site and hyperfine interactions calculations, aiding the interpretation of $\mu$SR experiments in magnetic materials.
